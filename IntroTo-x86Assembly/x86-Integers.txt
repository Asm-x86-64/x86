###(x86 Integers)

1.Integer constant is made up of an optional lead in sign one or more digits and an optional suffix.

        + or - digits radix

2.Sign and radix are optional.

3.Radix indicates the numbers base

S.No    Radix Character            Radix Value

1.      H                           Hexadecimal

2.      r                           Encoded real

3.      q/o                         Octal

4.      t                           Decimal

5.      y/b                         Binary

Example:-

1.26d -> d indicates 26 is Decimal(Base 10).

2.1001110b -> b indicates 1001110 is Binary(Base 2).

4.Integer expressions are only be evaluted at assembly time.(Before Execution)
